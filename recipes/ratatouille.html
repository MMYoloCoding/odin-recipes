Ratatouille

Description

in this simple tutorial, we'll use real rat to cook this delicious Ratatouille

Ingredients

  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • ¼ cup minced garlic
  • ¾ cup water
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 rat
  • 1 zucchini, trimmed and very thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Spread tomoato paste on the 2 rats
  3. Put the rat mixture into the microwave
  4. Put the rest of the Ingredients into mixture
